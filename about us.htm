We, Dailyart, led by an excellent team, consists of the trading company Jiangmen Victory Limited and the 6 Factories, are located in Jiangmen city, one of the biggest sanitary ware and hardware manufacturing bases of China. It only takes one and a half hours from Guangzhou to Jiangmen by car, and two hours from Hong Kiong to Jiangmen by ferry boat.
We are strong in stainless steel products of waste bin,ashtray,umbrella stand and bathroom accessories etc. Also we can offer you various stylish houseware, which range from household items, kitchenware and garden accessories. Besides our own manufactured items, hundreds of partner factories have been supplying their products to us to broaden our product range, which enables us to meet customers diversified needs and makes consolidating different ranges of products in one order possible. Additionally, we have interests in a joint-venture container dock, able handle up to 1,500,000 cargo containers per year.
With unique market insights and research update on market trends, our R&D team is dedicated to develop innovative, stylish and practical products so as to keep pace with the changing market trends. Furthermore, many OEM projects have been proved to be successful with our efforts. Our QC team carries out strictest quality control on every order. In this way, we are able to find out and solve the problems at the earliest stage, so as to ensure a minimum defect rate and best quality in accordance to customers needs.
In continuing efforts to provide the best service to a diverse range of customers, our experienced staffs are always ready to provide efficient, professional, and prompt response to customers queries.
